Its tuna pasta what more do you want me to say?

We're gonna put some sweetcorn and cucumber in it too, fasten your seatbelts.

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Pasta
  • Sweetcorn
  • cucumber
  • mayo

Steps

  1. Cook your pasta.
  2. Cut your cucumber into slices and then each slice into quarters.
  3. Rinse your sweetcorn.
  4. Once your pasta is cooked add your tuna and mayo to it and mix.
  5. Now add your sweetcorn and cucumber and, you guessed it, mix it.
  6. Enjoy.
