Chickpea Stir-fry

Description

This stir-fry is a meal i personally like to cook because of its simplicity, great taste, and vegetable protein source. Full of different vegetables and egg noodles this is a very filling meal.

A nice touch to go with this meal is an oven baked baguette with butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 Red pepper
  • 1 Yellow pepper
  • Courgette
  • Long beans
  • Snap peas
  • Mushrooms
  • Sliced water chestnuts
  • 1 Tin of chickpeas
  • Chinese spice to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Sweet chili sauce

Steps

  1. Cut your vegetables into desired sizes.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large pan or preferably a wok.
  3. Once the wok and oil has heated to a medium tempurature, add the cut peppers, courgette, long beans and snap peas. Fry for 5 minutes stirring regularly.
  4. Now add the mushrooms, water chestnuts and chickpeas, continue to stir for another 5 minutes.
  5. Add the chinese spice, salt and pepper to taste and continue stirring for another 5 minutes.
  6. Add your sauce and stir to coat all the ingredients.
  7. Fry until all vegetables are cooked through.
  8. Enjoy.
